## Production Notes

### File Preparation (Before Laser)
1. Open in LightBurn/RDWorks
2. Check dimensions (resize to 40-60mm for AED 1 items)
3. Verify kerf offset (0.1mm for 3mm MDF)
4. Remove duplicate lines
5. Nest tightly for batch cutting
6. Use vector scoring, NOT raster engraving

## 8. Quality Control Warning (Odor & Material)

Since products route through a local Dubai hub for quality checks, enforce these rules:

**Critical Instruction:** Low-cost Chinese synthetic leathers (PU) and plastics can carry a strong chemical smell. Since the brand position is "affordable essentials" with a clean aesthetic, a harsh chemical smell will destroy user trust.

**Action:**
- Request odor-free, eco-friendly glue and high-grade synthetic materials from the factory
- Request factory samples for smell-testing before placing bulk orders
- Include a QC checklist at the Dubai hub: visual match, functional test, odor check, packaging integrity
